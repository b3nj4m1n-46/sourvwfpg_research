# SOURVWF Research

Workforce development research materials for Southern Oregon / Rogue Valley, organized by geographic scope.

## Structure

| Folder | Contents |
|--------|----------|
| `Federal/` | Federal-level workforce policy: WIOA Pell, Federal Register notices, US Census migration data and white papers |
| `RV Specific/` | Rogue Valley region: economic indicators, occupational/wage projections (2024–2034), STEM employment, Rogue Workforce Partnership governance documents |
| `Statewide/` | Oregon-wide: WIOA State Combined Plan, Talent Assessments, Workforce Readiness reports, all 9 local board plans (2024–2028 + 2026 modifications), CTE skill statements, and HECC WIOA dashboard CSV extracts |
| `links.md` | Index of external research sources, with sub-indexes in each scope folder |

## Notable extracts

- **[Statewide/oregon-wioa-dashboard/](Statewide/oregon-wioa-dashboard/)** — structured CSV extracts of HECC's WIOA Title I Workforce Dashboard (2023) for both statewide and Rogue Workforce Partnership. See its [README](Statewide/oregon-wioa-dashboard/README.md) and [GAPS.md](Statewide/oregon-wioa-dashboard/GAPS.md) for what was captured and what's still to do.
